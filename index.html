How It Works

The Pomodoro technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

These intervals are usually done in groups of 3-4 at a time, including the short breaks, with a long break after the final interval.

Use Quick Setup to customize your session.
